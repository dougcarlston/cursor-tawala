Please send whatever you find to [your email address], or reach out if a different transfer method would be easier for larger files (I'm happy to set up a shared Drive folder or similar).

I really appreciate any help you can offer — your work on the Designer was central to what made Tawala work, and having even a partial recovery of that codebase would save months of reconstruction time.

Warm regards,
Doug Carlston
