Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tawala?

Tawala enables ordinary people to use the web more easily for organizing and communicating with groups.

How do I use Tawala?

Ready-made web apps can be personalized and shared via a link in email or on your website. Respondents fill out forms; Tawala processes and organizes the information.

What is the Tawala apps library?

A collection of free templates you can try, customize, and deploy to your My Tawala space. See Library.

How much does Tawala cost?

Featured solutions on the home page are free. Creating your own apps requires a free account — sign up here.
